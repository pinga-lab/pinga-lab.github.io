About

I am Lecturer in Geophysics at the Department of Earth, Ocean and Ecological Sciences of the University of Liverpool, UK. My research focuses on inverse problems in potential field geophysics with a heavy dose of open-source software development. I do my best to bring more openness to the scientific process and to train the next generation of scientists in the best practices of reproducible computational experiments.

In the spirit of open-science, I share all of the research and teaching material online under permissive licenses. You'll find links to source code, images, lessons, etc, throughout my personal website.

Follow me on Twitter (@leouieda) and Github (@leouieda) for updates on teaching and research, random things from the world of academia, etc.
