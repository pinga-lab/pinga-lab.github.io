About

My research focuses on inverse problems in potential field geophysics with a heavy dose of open-source software development. I do my best to bring more openness to the scientific process and to train the next generation of scientists in the best practices of reproducible computational experiments. I'm leading the Computer-Oriented Geoscience Lab, PINGA's sister lab and a virtual research group where we design computational methods for geophysical data processing, forward modeling, and inversion.
