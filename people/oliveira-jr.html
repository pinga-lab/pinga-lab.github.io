About

Researcher at the department of Geophysics of Observatório Nacional, Brazil

Research

I am mainly interested in Potential Fields, Inverse Problems and Numerical Methods.

Education

After obtaining my undergraduate degree in Geophysics at the Instituto de Astronomia, Geofísica e Ciências Atmosféricas da Universidade de São Paulo (IAG-USP), I received a master degree in Geophysics at Observatório Nacional where I subsequently obtained a PhD degree. Nowadays I am a researcher at the department of Geophysics of Observatório Nacional.
