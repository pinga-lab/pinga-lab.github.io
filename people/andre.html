About

Researcher at the Applied Geology Department of the Geology College (DGAP/FGEL) of the University of the Rio de Janeiro State (UERJ), Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Education

I'm graduated on Physics at Rio de Janeiro State University (UERJ) and I did a master's degree at National Observatory (ON). I have also obtained a PhD degree at ON. I'm interested in modelling and inversion of Potential Fields and Paleomagnetism.
